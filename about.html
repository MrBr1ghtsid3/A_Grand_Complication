Frequently Asked Questions

What is this place all about?

Good question! This is an outward expression of my inward desire to write and self-publish a work of fiction. I will have regular updates on my blog, as well as social media, going forward. See the hamburger.

Do you have a plot or a summary I can check?

I've only this vague idea about an ordinary hero stuck in time, struggling to move on. The world itself is quite absurd, replete with symbolism and metaphor. Each death is an opportunity to start over, but fragments of memories are forever lost in the process.

And who are you, again?

My name is Tsvetoslav Shalev and I dabble in creative writing. More on me, you can find on my blog page. See the hamburger.

I am sold! Can I pre-order?

Nah, not yet. As I get closer to something of a first draft, I'll make sure to update the site, accordingly. Think 2022. I'm probably being overly-optimistic.

What if I have suggestions or recommendations?

Oh, please get in touch, either via the Contact page, or through my Social Media links. See the hamburger. Thanks in advance!
